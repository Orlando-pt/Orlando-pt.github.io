About

Hello! I am Orlando Macedo. Software Developer, a DevOps Enthusiast and occasionally Photographer.

I'm a person who enjoys helping others, so the development of software is something that makes a lot of sense for me to do. Technology has greater potential to change people's lives for the better, and that's my main focus, provide good products to facilitate people's daily lives. Web development using microservices is something that feels very natural to me. Also, the new technologies associated with the subject bring me a lot of excitement to learn because of the new possibilities that they bring to the table. In addition to development, I particularly like to deploy and test applications using the variety of technologies available. Finally, I am a person who values physical and mental health a lot, that's where photography and physical exercise come to place, this two help me stay healthy, allowing me to be the best version of myself when it counts.
